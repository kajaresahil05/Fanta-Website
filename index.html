FANTA

FLAVOUR UPDATED

Fanta has recently refreshed its classic orange drink with a bolder, fruitier flavour and updated packaging to appeal to younger consumers. The brand is also expanding its range by introducing zero-sugar options and new fruity variants like Raspberry, Apple and Tutti-Frutti in different markets. These updates aim to keep the drink exciting, modern, and healthier-choice friendly. Worldwide, Fanta now offers many flavours beyond orange, reflecting changing tastes. Overall, the flavour upgrade is part of Fanta’s effort to stay fresh, fun and relevant for today’s customers.
